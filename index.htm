Fazal Yameen has led product and tech teams for over a decade. He was most recently the VP of Technology at Itemize, an expense management platform utilizing artificial intelligence (AI). Prior to that he was the founder and CTO of CabEasy, a taxi and car-sharing app which was selected by SOS Ventures for an intensive 3-month accelerator program in Europe. He spent several years leading product and tech at comiXology - creators of the Marvel and DC Comics apps – and was acting CTO during a 6-month search for a replacement. He grew the tech team 10x while leading a 20x increase in revenue. Prior to comiXology, he led product and tech at GPShopper, a mobile platform company that creates apps for Fortune 500 retailers including Best Buy and Express. He holds a BA in English and Computer Science from Wesleyan University.
